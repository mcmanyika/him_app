About Us

We are a bible believing church based in Zimbabwe, led by Apostle T. and Pastor C. Vutabwashe. We have a passion to identify, develop and celebrate skills among people across the globe. We would love to have you join us for worship every Sunday in any of our locations near you.

Our Vision

To perpertuate the ministry of Jesus christ on earth by obeying the great commission balancing high spirituality and high professionalism creating a home for all races where gifts and skills are discovered, developed and celebrated.
